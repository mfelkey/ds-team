## PDB-02 StatsBomb WC2026 xG Evaluation (ID: DS-2962256D)

### EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The evaluation of StatsBomb Open Data for use in training the Dixon-Coles model for the 2026 World Cup (WC2026) indicates that while it offers rich shot-level event data, there are gaps and limitations in historical coverage. The recommendation is Option 1 - using a club proxy via Understat as the primary source due to its comprehensive historical depth across multiple seasons which aligns better with Dixon-Coles requirements.

### DETAILED EVALUATION

#### Option 1: StatsBomb Open Data
- **Data Quality and Completeness**: 
    - The data provides detailed shot-level events for WC2018 and WC2022, including xG values. However, the completeness is limited to these two tournaments only.
- **Coverage**:
    - All matches from WC2018 and WC2022 are included, but there’s no historical data extending beyond these events.
- **Dixon-Coles Requirements**: 
    - The Dixon-Coles model requires a minimum of 3-5 seasons or equivalent tournaments. While the xG values at shot level are available for the two World Cups, this is insufficient to meet the requirement for historical depth.

#### Option 2: Club Proxy via Understat
- **Data Quality and Completeness**:
    - Understat offers comprehensive data including historical xG values at a detailed level.
- **Coverage**: 
    - Extensive coverage across multiple seasons from major leagues and competitions, providing sufficient historical depth to meet Dixon-Coles model requirements.
- **Dixon-Coles Requirements**:
    - Meets the requirement for historical xG by team and match with adequate data spanning at least 3-5 seasons.

#### Option 3: API-Football
- **Data Quality and Completeness**: 
    - Provides detailed historical data, but there are concerns about consistency in coverage over multiple seasons.
- **Coverage**:
    - Comprehensive in terms of matches covered but lacks the depth needed for Dixon-Coles.
- **Dixon-Coles Requirements**:
    - While API-Football offers rich data, it does not meet the necessary historical depth required by the Dixon-Coles model.

### RECOMMENDATION

**GO Option 2 (Club Proxy via Understat)**: 
Understat provides the necessary historical depth and comprehensive xG values at a detailed level, which aligns well with the requirements of the Dixon-Coles model. The data quality is consistent across multiple seasons, ensuring reliable inputs for our models.
